<h2>Refund & Cancellation Policy</h2>

At MOTTO, we strive to deliver high-quality services that meet and exceed our clients’ expectations. However, we understand that situations may arise where a refund may be requested. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions applicable to refunds.

<h3>Refund & Cancellation Policy regarding Feature development and project based priced professional services</h3>
<strong>1. General Terms</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Refund requests must be submitted in writing within 7 days of the issue arising.</li>
    <li>Refund eligibility is subject to the terms outlined below and is determined at the sole discretion of MOTTO.</li>
</ul>

<strong>2. Refund Eligibility</strong>
<p>Refunds may be granted under the following circumstances:</p>

<strong>a. Project Cancellation by the Client</strong>
<ul>
    <li>If the project is canceled by the client before work has commenced, a full refund may be issued, minus any administrative fees and or deposits.</li>
    <li>If the project is canceled after work has commenced, the refund will be calculated based on the stage of project completion and any work already delivered.</li>
</ul>

<strong>b. Failure to Deliver as Agreed</strong>
<ul>
    <li>If MOTTO fails to deliver services as outlined in the signed agreement or scope of work, the client may be eligible for a partial or full refund.</li>
    <li>The client must provide evidence of unmet deliverables.</li>
</ul>

<strong>c. Dissatisfaction with Delivered Work</strong>
<ul>
    <li>If the client is dissatisfied with the work delivered, MOTTO will work to resolve the issue through revisions as outlined in the agreement.</li>
    <li>Refunds for dissatisfaction will only be considered after all reasonable attempts to resolve the matter have been exhausted.</li>
</ul>

<strong>3. Non-Refundable Conditions</strong>
<p>Refunds will not be issued under the following conditions:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Work has been completed and approved by the client.</li>
    <li>Dissatisfaction arises due to factors outside of MOTTO's control, such as third-party tools or integrations.</li>
    <li>The client has delayed the project timeline without reasonable communication.</li>
    <li>The client has breached the terms of the agreement.</li>
</ul>

<strong>4. Refund Processing</strong>

<ul>
    <li>Approved refunds will be processed within 10 business days.</li>
    <li>Refunds will be issued using the original payment method unless otherwise agreed upon.</li>
</ul>

<strong>5. Dispute Resolution</strong>

<ul>
    <li>In case of disputes, both parties agree to make reasonable efforts to resolve the issue amicably. If a resolution cannot be reached, the matter may be escalated to arbitration as per the terms of the agreement.</li>
</ul>

<strong>6. Amendments to the Policy</strong>

<ul>
    <li>MOTTO reserves the right to update or amend this refund policy at any time. Changes will not affect projects initiated before the policy update.</li>
</ul>

<p>For any questions or refund requests, please <a href="/contact/">contact us</a></p>

<h2>Refund & Cancellation Policy for Subscription-Based Service Contracts</h2>
<p>At MOTTO, we are committed to delivering value through our subscription-based services. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds applicable to subscription agreements.</p>

<strong>1. General Terms</strong>
<ul>
    <li>This policy applies to all subscription-based services offered by MOTTO.</li>
    <li>Refund requests must be submitted in writing to admin@motto.ca within the timelines specified below.</li>
    <li>Refund eligibility is determined based on the conditions outlined in this policy.</li>
</ul>

<strong>2. Refund Eligibility</strong>

<strong>a. Initial Subscription Period (First Payment)</strong>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Full Refund:</strong> If the client cancels within 3 days of the initial subscription payment and no substantial work or deliverables have been provided, a full refund may be issued.</li>
    <li><strong>Partial Refund:</strong> If work has commenced and/or deliverables have been provided, a partial refund may be issued, deducting costs for work already completed.</li>
</ul>

<strong>b. Subsequent Subscription Payments</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Refunds for subsequent payments will not be issued unless:</li>
    <ul>
        <li>The client cancels before the renewal date.</li>
        <li>The agency fails to provide agreed-upon services during the billing cycle.</li>
    </ul>
</ul>

<strong>c. Overcharges or Duplicate Payments</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Refunds for overcharges or duplicate payments will be processed upon verification within 10 business days.</li>
</ul>

<strong>3. Non-Refundable Conditions</strong>
<p>Refunds will not be provided under the following conditions:</p>
<ul>
    <li>The client has consumed services or deliverables for the billing cycle.</li>
    <li>Dissatisfaction arises due to factors beyond MOTTO’s control, such as changes in third-party services or client-provided materials.</li>
    <li>The client fails to cancel the subscription before the next billing cycle.</li>
</ul>

<strong>4. Cancellation Policy</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Clients may cancel their subscription at any time by writing an email to admin@motto.ca</li>
    <li>Cancelation is effective at the end of the current billing cycle, and no refunds will be issued for the remaining time in the cycle unless eligible under <strong>Refund Eligibility</strong>.</li>
</ul>

<strong>5. Refund Processing</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Approved refunds will be processed within 10 business days to the original payment method.</li>
    <li>Refunds may incur administrative fees of up to 15% of the refund amount.</li>
</ul>

<strong>6. Service Modifications</strong>
<ul>
    <li>If MOTTO modifies the service significantly, and the client is dissatisfied, the client may request a refund for the current billing cycle within 10 days of the modification.</li>
</ul>

<strong>7. Dispute Resolution</strong>
<ul>
    <li>Both parties agree to resolve disputes amicably. If no resolution is reached, the issue may be escalated to mediation or arbitration as outlined in the service agreement.</li>
</ul>

<strong>8. Policy Amendments</strong>
<ul>
    <li>MOTTO reserves the right to update this refund policy. Clients will be notified of changes, and the updated policy will apply to future billing cycles only.</li>
</ul>
<p>For further assistance or to request a refund, please <a href="/contact/">contact us</a>.</p>
